Sitename

Red Mountain Llama and Alpaca Petting Zoo

Domain name: redmountainpettingzoo.org

This name represents the organization's goal, and location as two handy tools for quick understanding of the purpose of this page.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to give information about the Red Mountain Llama and Alpaca Petting Zoo, such as what animals are there, what times they are open, prices of admission and contact info for further questions and inquires.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the Red Mountain Llama and Alpaca Petting Zoo?
  • What animals are there?
  • When is it open from, and how much will it cost?
