About Us

The Red Mountain Llama and Alpaca Petting Zoo started as a simple small farm at the base of the Red Mountains in the early 1940s. We cared for and housed many different animals, and especially a large quantity of llamas and alpacas. Our Petting Zoo is visible just off of the highway, so kids always wanted to stop by to see them and pet them, so we figured we would make a safe and fun way for everyone to come experience the joys of animals and all the wonderful things they do.
